Chess Piece Notation

Each Piece is Notated with a Capital Letter Except for the Pawn. The Pawn is notated by the File/Rank of its square.

♔

King

Notation: K

♕

Queen

Notation: Q

♗

Bishop

Notation: B

♘

Knight

Notation: N (not K)

♖

Rook

Notation: R

♙

Pawn

Notation: no letter

Quick Practice

Which piece uses 'N' instead of 'K'?

The Knight uses 'N' because 'K' is used for the King

How do we write a pawn move?

Just write the square (e.g., e4, d5) - no piece letter needed
