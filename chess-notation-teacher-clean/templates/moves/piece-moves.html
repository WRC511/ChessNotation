Basic Piece Moves

When a piece (except a pawn) moves to a square, we write:

Piece Letter + Destination Square

For example: Ke4 means the King moves to square e4

♔
→
Ke4

Remember:

  • Use the piece's letter (K for King, Q for Queen, B for Bishop, N for Knight, R for Rook)
  • Then write the destination square (like e4, f6, etc.)
  • The notation doesn't show where the piece came from, only where it's going

How would you write: "Bishop moves to square f6"?
