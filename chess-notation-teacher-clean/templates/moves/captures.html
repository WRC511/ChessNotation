Capture Moves

When a piece captures another piece, we write:

Piece Letter + x + Destination Square

For example: Bxe4 means Bishop captures on square e4

♗
⚔️
Bxe4

Remember:

  • Use the piece's letter (K, Q, B, N, R)
  • Add an 'x' to show it's a capture
  • Then write the destination square
  • We don't write what piece was captured

Practice Question

How would you write: "Queen captures on square f7"?
