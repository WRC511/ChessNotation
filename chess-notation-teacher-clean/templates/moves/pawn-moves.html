Pawn Moves

When a pawn moves forward to a square, we write:

Just the Destination Square

For example: e4 means a pawn moves to square e4

♙
→
e4

Remember:

  • Pawns are the only pieces that don't use a letter in their notation
  • Just write the destination square (like e4, d5, etc.)
  • Pawns can only move forward (unless capturing)
